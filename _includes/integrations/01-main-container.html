Salesforce

PowerBox has integration with Salesforce.com in many objects. Take case for example, customer supports are able to communicate with customers directly through file sharing and chatting. Large files like logs can also be storage in PowerBox without worrying about Salesforce storage limits. Customers can simply upload the logs to the URL supports shared and when the files are being uploaded, supports will receive notifications immediately. Further handle the case as soon to largely reduce time spent on sending emails back and forth.
